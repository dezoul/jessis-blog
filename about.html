The Story Behind Jessie Adventures Around

I’m Jessie, the creative voice behind Jessie Adventures Around! 

I’ve spent the last 7 years traveling and living around the globe everywhere from the USA, to Europe, Asia, and back again.  For me, travel has been a life lesson on mindset.  My brain shifted gears into an adventure mode and suddenly, a whole new world was opened up to me!  I was examining parts of myself to see what was really an essential part of my being and what was given to me by society.  I started trying new foods and sleeping in strange places.  I was communicating with people without language and learning to accept customs as different, not right or wrong.  I learned to view religions as cultural interpretations of the same basic message: Be good.  That’s my favorite thing about travel, it gets you so far out of your comfort zone, you create a whole new comfort zone…and then realize that even that comfort zone is limiting you!  It’s all about that perspective shift.  Once I realized how my limiting beliefs and my limited knowledge was shaping my future by influencing almost every decision I made, my whole life changed.  My definition of success and happiness changed; and THAT changed my life.

  I am so passionate about helping others bust out of their comfort zones and pursue the life of their dreams!  My approach to pursuing goals is a lot like my approach to travel - choose where you want to go, figure out a way to get there, and make sure you do everything you can to enjoy the trip!  I make finding the joy along the journey a big goal because travel taught me to be present and enjoy your situation for what it is at any given moment.  That isn’t to say that there isn’t hard work and long days involved, but ask any traveler why they put up with the headaches… it’s because the trip is always worth the difficult things it takes to get there.  Goals are a lot like that; hard to get to, but worth it in the end!

When I moved back to the United States for the first time, I had a completely different definition of what success looked like for my life, so I decided to be bold and figure out a new career path that would allow me the flexibility to travel and live life on my terms.  I had already boldly quit my career in genetic research to live in Italy for three years, why not be bold again and just chase a new career that was more aligned with my new definition of success? 

That’s exactly what I did and I want to help you do the same thing for yourself. 

Unless you do something to get yourself into your own version of a dream life, it will remain just that - a dream life, not your real life.  You can set aside your limiting beliefs, acquire the skills and knowledge you need to pursue a career path or second income stream that allows you to move towards your dream life.  I did, and I was no different than you are right now! 

The truth is, finding what you want out of life and then having the courage to chase it is something only you can do.  It takes some inspiration, a little guidance, a lot of courage, but you are the only one who can do it for yourself… but that doesn’t mean you are meant to do it by yourself! 

The Jessie Adventures Around community is all about supporting each other as we each chase our own unique dreams.  For daily tips, hacks, and inspiration, weekly guest speakers and special access to group coaching challenges, become a part of this supportive community
